What is Low Grain Refrigerant (LGR)?

Low Grain Refrigerant (LGR) Dehumidifiers have the capacity to remove moisture and water vapour more effectively than your conventional residential/commercial grade dehumidifier. The efficiency of LGR Dehumidifiers are capable of achieving and operating at relative humidity levels as low as 20%.
